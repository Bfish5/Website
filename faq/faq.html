About Finesse Web Solutions

Why We Exist

Finesse Web Solutions exists for one reason: to help small businesses stop losing customers because of bad or non-existent websites.

We focus on businesses like plumbers, barbers, contractors, cleaners, home services, and local shops all over the U.S. — businesses that are great at what they do, but don’t have the time or desire to mess with web design and tech.

Our style is clean, intriguing, and modern. We keep things simple, focus on clarity, and build sites that look professional without being overcomplicated.

You handle the work you’re great at. We handle the website that helps bring you more of that work.

How We Work With You

We don’t disappear for weeks or bury you in jargon. Instead, we:

  • • Talk to you like a real person, not a tech manual.
  • • Build a live demo by the next day.
  • • Edit your site with you on Zoom, in real time.
  • • Only launch when you’re happy with how it looks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Straight Answers
  • What if I don’t have any photos or text yet?
    That’s okay. We can start with simple placeholder text and stock-style images, then refine as you gather more content. The most important thing is getting a professional, working website live.
  • Do I really get free hosting forever?
    Yes. You pay a one-time fee of $1,000 for the build, and hosting is included. You don’t pay us a monthly hosting fee.
  • Do you help with the domain name?
    Yes. You’ll purchase your custom domain (like yourbusiness.com) from a provider such as GoDaddy. We’ll connect that domain to your new website so everything works smoothly.
  • What if I want changes after launch?
    During the Zoom session, we’ll work with you until you like how your site looks. Small adjustments after launch are easy — just reach out and we’ll take care of it.
  • Can you build more than 3 pages?
    Yes. The base offer is a 3-page website, but we can add more pages for an additional cost if your business needs them.
  • How fast can you have everything ready?
    Typically, your website is designed, edited with you, and launched within 1–2 days of the first call.
  • How do I get started?
    Simply call or text 443-797-2249. We’ll schedule a quick intro call, plan your site, and set up your next-day demo.
