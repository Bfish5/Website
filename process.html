The 6-Step Process

Simple & Stress-Free
01
Quick Intro Call

Call or text 443-797-2249 for a 5–10 minute conversation. We learn what you do, where you’re located, and what you want your website to accomplish.

02
We Plan Your Site

We outline your 3 pages — usually Home, Services/About, and Contact — and decide what needs to be front and center: services, photos, pricing, and calls to action.

03
Next-Day Demo

By the next day, we build a demo version of your site so you can see exactly how it will look and flow, before anything goes live.

04
Live Zoom Edit Session

We hop on a Zoom call with you, walk through the demo, and make edits in real time until it feels like a perfect fit for your business.

05
Final Approval

We don’t launch your website until you like how it looks. Once you approve, we’re ready to flip the switch.

06
Launch Within Minutes

After approval, we publish your site. In minutes, your business has a clean, modern, live website that customers can actually use.

Most projects are fully completed in 1–2 days from the first call.
